Callout — a calm tinted inset for bench reminders and "Don't confuse it with…" traps.

```jsx
<Callout eyebrow="Don't confuse it with" tone="caution">
  H₂S — that's the black precipitate along the stab, not a crack.
</Callout>
```

Tones: `neutral`, `info` (teal), `success` (sage), `caution` (terracotta), `gold`. Set `onDark` when placed on a deep-teal answer slide. Keep copy short and bench-specific.
