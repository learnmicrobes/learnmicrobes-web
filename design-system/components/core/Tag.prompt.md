Bench-finding chip — a white pill with a leading dot for stating evidence (media, hemolysis, morphology, key tests).

```jsx
<Tag>Sheep blood agar</Tag>
<Tag accent="gramPos">GPC in chains</Tag>
<Tag onDark>β-hemolytic</Tag>
```

`accent` colors the dot (`gramPos`, `gramNeg`, `anaerobe`, `biochem`, `caution`) to carry clinical meaning. `onDark` for green surfaces; `dot={false}` to drop the dot.
