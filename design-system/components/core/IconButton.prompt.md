IconButton — a compact square control for nav/toolbar icons; wraps a Font Awesome solid glyph and always takes an accessible `label`.

```jsx
<IconButton label="Toggle dark mode" variant="onDark">
  <i className="fa-solid fa-moon" />
</IconButton>
```

Variants: `soft` (sunken warm tile, app default), `solid` (teal), `ghost`, `onDark` (translucent white on the teal header bar). Sizes `sm | md | lg`. Use for the header sign-in/menu/theme controls and card-level actions.
